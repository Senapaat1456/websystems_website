MUDAC

MUDAC is a data science competion that DAA comepetes in each year. MUDAC stands for Midwest Data Analytics Competion. The way it words is a the teams meet at a choosen university, recive the data they are going to be working with in the morning, and then have around 24 hours to analize the data and write a presenation exsplaining what they found from the data. In 2020, due to the Covid-19 outbreak, the competion was done virtually. In 2019 one of the DAA teams took first place out of all the teams.
